Is the location of a child stored or shared with other devices than parent's device?
No. Everytime you want to display the location of a child by tapping on Tap here to update location on the top right corner of , the parent's device sends a request to the child's device to share its location. Once the location is obtained by the GPS, the child's device will send its location only to the parent device that requested the location. Once the location is shown on the app, the app does not store the location. All requested locations are only shared with devices that requested such location. As a security measure, each account has a private code, only know to the app, which works as a signature to each request. Without such signature, locations are not displayed or submited. Additionally, locations are sent through secure channels using TLS protocol.
Why is the app asking for permission to store data?
If the app is asking for permission to store data, access photos and/or media in your device, probably by seeing or , is because you are probably using the app as a parent and the app needs permission to store data in order to download and save map images to display a location. Without this permission location of a child will not be displayed because the app is not able to store the needed map images to draw a location on the screen.

In order to show the location of a child, the user has to grant this permission when the app asks for it or grant it manually by going to Settings -> Apps -> Child Locator -> Permissions on your device.
How to grant/reject location permission to the app?
If you are using the app as a child, the app needs access the device location in order to send it to a parent device when requested. Usually the app asks for this permission to the user when using it as a child as in where you can select the appropiate permission. If your GPS is off the app will ask you to turn it on as in .

To manually grant/reject location permission to the app you can go to Settings -> Apps -> Child Locator -> Permissions -> Location on your device and get a screen like where you can set the corresponding permission.

To manually turn on/off your GPS you can go to Settings -> Location on your device.
A child location is not being shown
This issue can be fixed with the following options
  • By following the steps in How can I set the app to display a location? above.
  • By making sure both parent device and child device have Internet access.
  • By making sure that the parent's device has permission to store data, see Why is the app asking for permission to store data? above.
  • By making sure that the GPS on the child's device is on and the app has the permission to use it, see How to grant/reject location permission to the app? above.
How can I see the location of more than one child?
When downloading the app, you have a trial period of 30 days to check its funcitonality and it is restricted to display only the location of one child. In case you like the app and would like to check more than one location you can subscribe to the service by going on the parent's device to the settings menu on the top right corner of and then select Service subscription on to set the subscription.

Once you are subscribed to the service, you can add an unlimited number of children to check their locations on one place like in .
How can I stop sharing the location of a device?
The suggested way to stop sharing a location by the app in a child's device is by logging out from it. To do this, you can go to the child's device, open the app and tap on logout on and then input your account password on . Once the password is verified, which requires Internet connection, the app stops the process of getting and sharing a location. We require password verification to let parents have control over the app and avoid children to accidentally stop sharing a location from the app.

Note that you can also stop sharing the location of a device by rejecting the location permission granted to the app as explained in How to grant/reject location permission to the app? above.
The location displayed by the app is not accurate
The location obtained by a device, and therefore the location displayed on the app, could be inaccurate in some cases and this has nothing to do with the app but instead with the device and the environment in which is the device is located. An accurate location can be achieved with the following
  • Set your device to get an accurate location instead of an approximate location: In some devices, you can specify this if you go to Settings -> Location to get a screen like in which you can select mode option and get to to select a high accuracy location.
  • Be in an open space, near a window or in a place in which a satellite can determine the position of a device. See the global positioning system (GPS) to learn how the positioning system works and GPS accuracy to see what aspects may affect its accuracy. In particular, GPS-enabled smartphones are typically accurate to within a 4.9 m (16 ft.) radius under open sky. However, their accuracy worsens near buildings, bridges, and trees.
  • By making multiple location requests to the app: Once a location is displayed, you can request the location again for few more times in order to get a more accurate location.
